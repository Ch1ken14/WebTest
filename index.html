Game Projects

Bonkers

Bonkers is a multiplayer "SmashBro-Like" game with a mix of Apple Picker. The Players will have to compete with each other to get the most amount of coins while trying to knock the other player of the stage.

Unadorned Tower Defense

Unadorned Tower Defense is a simple tower defense game set in a medieval era. The player must protect the kingdom from an endless horde of the undead by building towers, upgrading, and collecting resources.

Blossoming Memories

Blossoming Memories is a Narrative Driven Simulation game. The player plays the character of a florist selling flowers to an unfamilliar neighborhood. Through out her journey, she will meet a variety of characters that helps her understand more about herself and about flowers.
